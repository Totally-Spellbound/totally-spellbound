# Tone of Voice

Voice: warm, knowledgeable shopkeeper with a mystical streak. Enchanting but honest.
Plain and kind, like Alison across the counter. Invites, never pressures. Pairs with
docs/brand.md + docs/sales.md.

Do:
- Open with feeling/story, then back it with facts.
- Speak to intention/use ("keep it by the bed for calm", "a gentle first crystal").
- Be sensory/specific (colour, size, weight in hand, finish) - builds trust, cuts returns.
- Briefly say what a stone is traditionally associated with, in plain words.
- Short paragraphs; optional facts bullets for scanning.
- Keep the shop present (hand-picked, hand-packed).

Don't:
- Hype stacking / ALL CAPS / exclamation spam.
- Pressure or fake scarcity (unless a real time-bound campaign).
- Medical/financial/guaranteed claims ("cures"/"will" -> "traditionally"/"carried for").
- Corporate filler.
- Em/en dashes (hyphens only); non-UK spelling.
- Parroting dropship manufacturer copy - rewrite, strip duplicate boilerplate (SEO).

Titles: searchable first, characterful second. Lead with the product noun a shopper
types. Pattern `[What it is] - [key descriptor/material/theme]`.
- Good: "Amethyst Tumbled Stone - Calm & Intuition"; "Black Tourmaline Point -
  Protection Crystal"; "Alchemy Gothic Batheart Pendant - The Vampire's Kiss".
- Avoid: keyword stuffing, ALL CAPS, vague poetry with no product noun.

Description shape:
1. Hook (1-2 sentences): feeling/story/use.
2. The piece (2-4): crystal, form, size, finish, colour, handmade/hand-picked.
3. Meaning (1-3): traditional associations tied to the product's Intention/Chakra/
   Element; plain, non-clinical.
4. One gentle use/gift nudge (see docs/sales.md).
5. Optional facts bullets: Crystal, Approx size, Finish, Handmade?, Care.

Check each rewrite: sounds like homepage not warehouse; no em/en dash, UK, £; no banned
claims; title leads with searchable noun; copy matches chosen metafields; helpful not
pushy.
