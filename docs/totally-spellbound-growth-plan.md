## Section 1: The Launch (Month 1)

### Week 1 — Sons set up the foundations (one evening)

- [ ] Set up Shopify store — theme, branding "Totally Spellbound", payment, shipping
- [ ] Configure flat rate shipping: £2.50 small items / £4.50 larger items / free over £30
- [ ] Install Xyla AI — connect Facebook + Instagram + TikTok
- [ ] Install Shopify POS app on Alison's phone
- [ ] Download Photoroom on Alison's phone
- [ ] Walk Alison through Sidekick (it's just chatting in plain English)
- [ ] Add a "Where to Find Us" page for craft fair schedule

### Week 1-2 — Alison adds all products (one or two Sundays in the shop)

**Per product workflow:**
1. Pick up the item, weigh it on the kitchen scale — note the weight
2. Place on white card by the window — take a photo on phone
3. Open Photoroom — tap to clean up the background (10 seconds)
4. Open Shopify app — add product: name, price, photo, weight, stock count
5. Tell Sidekick: "Write a description for this amethyst point, 3 inches tall, good for calm energy and meditation"
6. Sidekick writes the description — check it looks right — done
7. Next item

**This is also the stock take** — counting everything as it goes in. Within a day or two, every product is digitally tracked.

### From day one after products are added:

- Xyla AI immediately starts auto-generating and scheduling social media posts
- Alison reviews and approves them when she gets a moment
- Start using Shopify POS on phone for in-shop sales (tap instead of writing on paper)

### Handling special situations:

- **Cash sales in shop:** Tap items on POS, select "Cash" as payment method. Sale recorded, stock updated.
- **Custom/personalised orders in shop:** Use "Custom Sale" on POS — type what it was and the price. Recorded as a one-off.
- **Something sells in shop that's also online:** Tell Sidekick "reduce stock of [item] by 1" or POS does it automatically if rung through the app.

### Weekly routine after launch (~30 mins/week):

- Tap in-shop sales on phone instead of paper
- Check Xyla 2-3 times a week — approve queued posts (~10 mins)
- Check Shopify for online orders once a day (~5 mins)
- When an order comes in: wrap it (bubble wrap, padded envelope), write/print the address (Shopify shows it), drop at post office, mark as "shipped" in Shopify (one tap)

## Important Notes

- **GDPR:** Customer emails are collected at checkout for order confirmations. Marketing emails (new arrivals etc.) require the customer to tick an opt-in box. The box cannot be pre-ticked under UK law.
- **Royal Mail calculated rates:** Requires a third-party Shopify app (some free, some paid). Product weights are stored natively in Shopify so switching is easy when ready.
- **Etsy:** Ruled out due to high fees and poor customer service experience.
- **eBay:** Not in the initial plan but could be revisited later. Shopify can sync with eBay if desired.
