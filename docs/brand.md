# Brand

Totally Spellbound: real independent family-run witchy/gothic crystal & spiritual-gifts
shop. 108 Outram Street, Sutton-in-Ashfield, Notts NG17 4FS. Mon-Sat 10am-4pm, closed
Sun. Owner **Alison** hand-picks stock and makes laser-engraved / 3D-printed pieces
(differentiator). Sells crystals, tarot/oracle, incense, books, altar/home, gothic gifts
(e.g. Alchemy Gothic), ~£1-£65, plus imported dropship gothic stock (Kate's Clothing).

Promise: hand-picked crystals, tarot & curiosities from a real little shop - packed by
hand in the shop, not a warehouse.

Pillars (each product hits >=1):
1. Real shop, real person (not a warehouse; Alison chose it).
2. Meaning over merch ("every crystal holds a story"); sell intention/ritual/feeling.
3. Guided, never pushy.
4. Grounded mysticism (evocative but honest; no fake claims).
5. Independent & local (Nottinghamshire, small, family-run).

Vocabulary (use lightly): Enter the Vault, Consult the Oracle, Shop by Intention,
Written in the Stars, the Cauldron (cart), ritual, correspondences, kindred,
hand-picked, hand-packed, curiosities, wheel of the year.

Customer: mostly UK, crystals/tarot/gothic; from beginners to practitioners. Welcome
beginners without talking down to adepts.

Hard rules:
- No em/en dashes in customer copy - hyphens only.
- UK spelling; £ pricing.
- No medical/psychological/financial or guaranteed-outcome claims. Use "traditionally
  associated with" / "carried for", never "cures"/"will".
- Keep real details accurate (address, hours, handmade origin); invent nothing.
- Align copy with chosen metafields (docs/metafields.md).
- Don't claim "new"/"one-of-a-kind" unless true.

Voice samples (verbatim, live site): "Every crystal holds a story." / "Every order is
wrapped in our shop, not a warehouse." / "Helpful like a conversation in the shop -
never pushy."
