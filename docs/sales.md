# Sales / Conversion

Sell like a trusted independent shopkeeper: clarity + relevance + reassurance, never
pressure ("never pushy" is a brand promise). Pairs with docs/brand.md +
docs/tone-of-voice.md.

Levers (use):
- Relevance: match the product's Intention/zodiac to why they're shopping (strongest
  lever - store is organised by intention & sign).
- Concreteness: specific size/finish/colour reduces hesitation and returns.
- Use-case & gifting prompts (how to use / who to gift) raise value and basket size.
- Trust/provenance: real shop, hand-packed, Alison's handmade pieces vs faceless
  dropshippers.
- Honest urgency only: genuine one-of-a-kind, limited handmade runs, real wheel-of-year
  campaigns (Lammas, Samhain).
- Easy next step: clear CTA; mention Apple/Google Pay, Klarna, cards where natural.

Avoid: fake scarcity/urgency/stock counts; pressure language; guaranteed or medical/
financial claims (also UK legal risk); hype; fake "was" prices.

In a description: match intention early; turn one feature into a concrete felt benefit;
one gentle nudge near the end (not a command); reassure (hand-packed/real-shop/handmade);
for gifts suggest a recipient (zodiac/intention/occasion).

SEO-for-sales (traffic is the real bottleneck - prior site got 1 sale/year): put
searchable words (crystal name, "protection crystal", "tarot deck", "gothic pendant") in
title + first sentence; rewrite duplicate dropship copy to be unique (fixes Google
"discovered, not indexed"); write for the person, not keyword stuffing.

Check: real reason to buy (intention/gift/use)? exactly one gentle non-pushy nudge? all
claims honest/non-medical? searchable phrase in title + opening? any urgency actually
true?
