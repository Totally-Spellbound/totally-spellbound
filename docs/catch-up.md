# Catch-Up: Helping Mum Move Her Shop Online (Shopify)

## Context
We're helping a 65-year-old, non-tech-savvy mum set up and manage a Shopify store with minimal technical effort. She wants to sell products online and promote them on social media without needing to learn complex tools.

---

## 1. Shopify's Built-In AI: Sidekick

Shopify has a built-in AI assistant called **Sidekick** that lives inside the Shopify admin dashboard. She can type plain English to manage her store:

- "Add a new product called Blue Scarf, price $25"
- "Update the price of the Red Hat to $15"
- "Why are my sales down this week?"
- "Help me launch a product promotion"

**Sidekick Pulse** (Winter 2026 update) now proactively monitors her store and surfaces recommendations without her asking.

**Shopify Magic** is also built in — it auto-generates product descriptions, email subject lines, etc.

No extra apps or setup needed. Included with every Shopify plan.

**Key links:**
- https://www.shopify.com/sidekick
- https://www.shopify.com/magic
- https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/shopify-admin/productivity-tools/sidekick

## 2. Social Media Auto-Posting: Research Results

We did deep sentiment research (real user reviews from Shopify App Store, Trustpilot, Reddit, G2) on 5 Shopify apps that auto-generate social media posts from her products. Here's what we found:

### Final Ranking

#### #1: Xyla AI (RECOMMENDED)
- **Rating:** 4.8/5 (160 reviews)
- **Price:** Free (5 posts/mo) | $29/mo Growth (1 post/day) | $39/mo Pro (3 posts/day)
- **Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, TikTok, X (Pro), YouTube (Pro)
- **Why it won:**
  - True autopilot — picks products, generates posts, publishes automatically
  - Mixes in memes, quotes, holiday content — feed doesn't look like robot spam
  - Best ease-of-use across all reviews — multiple users say "2 minute setup"
  - NO billing horror stories (unlike competitors)
  - Responsive, helpful support
  - 90% five-star reviews
- **Plan:** Start free, upgrade to Growth ($29/mo) if she likes it
- **Link:** https://apps.shopify.com/xyla-ai

#### #2: Outfy (Runner-up)
- **Rating:** 4.8/5 (462 reviews)
- **Price:** Free (1 post/day/network) | $20/mo Starter | $40/mo Pro
- **Platforms:** IG, FB, Pinterest, TikTok, X, YouTube, Threads
- **Pros:** Most established (since 2015), screen-share support walkthroughs, free tier for testing
- **Cons:** Billing complaints (charges after cancellation), hidden "Smart Q" setting can cause posts to silently fail, app-branded tags on posts
- **Link:** https://apps.shopify.com/outfy

#### #3: AutoPost by Amasty (Budget option)
- **Rating:** 4.9/5 (only 12 reviews — too new)
- **Price:** Free (5 posts/mo) | $9.99/mo Basic | $15.99/mo Advanced
- **Platforms:** IG, FB, X, Pinterest (no TikTok/YouTube)
- **Pros:** Cheapest option, auto-posts when new products are added
- **Cons:** Only 12 reviews, one user had automation failures, limited platforms
- **Link:** https://apps.shopify.com/autopost-social-media-ai-post

#### #4: Predis AI (Too expensive for this use case)
- **Rating:** 4.7/5 (96 reviews)
- **Price:** Free (15 credits ~2 posts) | $19-32/mo Core (no auto-post!) | $40-79/mo Rise (auto-post starts here)
- **Platforms:** IG, FB, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Google
- **Pros:** Good AI content quality, strong support
- **Cons:** Auto-posting requires $40-79/mo (too pricey), confusing credit system, billing complaint about charges after account deletion
- **Link:** https://apps.shopify.com/predisai

#### #5: SocialPilot (AVOID)
- **Rating:** 2.5/5 (3 reviews)
- **Price:** $20/mo (14-day trial)
- **Cons:** 73% one-star reviews on Trustpilot, poor support, missing documentation, launched May 2025 and still immature
- **Link:** https://apps.shopify.com/socialpilot

## 3. The Workflow She'll Experience

```
1. Log into Shopify admin -> use Sidekick to manage products via plain English
2. Xyla AI (installed from Shopify App Store) auto-pulls her products
3. AI generates social media posts with images + captions + hashtags
4. Posts include a mix of product promos, memes, quotes, seasonal content
5. She reviews a queue/calendar of upcoming posts
6. She approves (or tweaks) -> posts go out on schedule
```

## 4. Setup Help Needed (One-Time)

Someone tech-comfortable should help her with:
- Initial Shopify store setup
- Connecting social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, etc.) to Xyla AI
- Picking a posting schedule in Xyla
- Walking her through the review/approve flow once

After that, she's self-sufficient — Sidekick for the store, Xyla for socials.
